Our
History

We are a company dedicated to the research and development of new technologies with an emphasis on cutting-edge applications and equipment for the Oil and Gas industry. Our development is made up of powerful software created with Artificial Intelligence (technology based on training data to learn and improve the accuracy of the acquired data) that, in symmetry with high-tech hardware, makes a first-class tool for real-time satellite monitoring of wells operated with a rocker arm system.

"Our software, based, among other things, on the acquisition of dynamic charts and essential parameters for field monitoring, is specifically designed to help obtain maximum performance from mechanical pumping systems."

By leveraging advanced data analysis and intelligent automation, our software offers benefits such as monitoring, analysis, and early detection of faults, issuing real-time alerts, as well as decision-making in the solution of any faults that may occur. In addition to this development, we are working on innovative steam injection equipment for oil wells. Our corporation is comprised of a team of top-level engineers and technicians in various areas whose goal is to produce products with the highest quality standards.
